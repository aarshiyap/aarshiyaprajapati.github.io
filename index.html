Welcome to my website, where I employ the power of data-driven insights and machine learning to predict health insurance claims. I've created this project to showcase my abilities for the Data Development and Application Development role for the IT internship at TruStage.

On the application side, I've skillfully utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to demonstrate my proficiency in creating engaging and interactive web experiences that align with TruStage's innovative approach. In addition, for the data prediction model, I've employed Python, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn to build a machine-learning solution that leverages these technologies to make accurate predictions. In my analysis, I harnessed the power of data-driven insights and machine learning to predict health insurance claims. I carefully considered a range of critical variables, including BMI, Blood Pressure, Number of Children, Age, Gender, Smoking Status, Diabetes Condition, and Region of Residence. My goal was to develop a predictive model that could estimate the expected health insurance claim amount based on an individual's unique set of these variables. The dataset I used for model training was sourced from Kaggle, a renowned data science platform, allowing us to leverage a wealth of real-world health insurance data for accurate predictions.
